Welcome to Triforce Tavern

Welcome to Triforce Tavern, a dining hall for heroes, wanderers, and royalty alike. Inspired by the world of The Legend of Zelda, our menu brings together hearty meals from across Hyrule — Goron-sized roasts, fresh Zora-inspired seafood, and sweet Lon Lon Ranch desserts. Whether you’re gathering your party before a great quest or simply looking to restore your hearts after a long day, Triforce Tavern is the place where courage, wisdom, and power come together in every bite.
